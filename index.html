About Company


Amrawati Associates is a leading construction firm owned by Tribhuwan Pandey, dedicated to providing comprehensive services in the real estate industry. With a rich history of excellence, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in property sales and purchase, construction work, interior design, architecture, and legal advisory services.

Our Commitment to Quality and Expertise


At Amrawati Associates, we understand that buying, selling, and constructing properties can be a complex and daunting task. That is why we strive to offer a one-stop solution for all your real estate needs. Whether you are looking to invest in a new property, sell your existing one, or construct your dream project from the ground up, our team of skilled professionals is here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Philosophy


At Amrawati Associates, we believe in building long-term relationships with our clients based on trust, integrity, and exceptional service. We are committed to delivering projects on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards. Our customer-centric approach ensures that we understand our clients' unique needs and provide tailored solutions to meet their requirements effectively.

Services

Construction

Amrawati Associates specializes in all types of construction projects, ranging from residential buildings to commercial complexes. Our team of experienced architects, engineers, and construction experts collaborates closely with clients to bring their visions to life. We adhere to the highest standards of quality, using superior materials and employing modern construction techniques to deliver outstanding results.

Interior Design and Architecture

We believe that a well-designed space enhances the overall living and working experience. Our skilled interior designers and architects work closely with clients to create functional and aesthetically pleasing interiors that reflect their unique preferences. From conceptualization to execution, we pay attention to every detail to ensure that the final design exceeds expectations.

Property Sales and Purchase

With our extensive network and in-depth knowledge of the local market, we assist clients in buying and selling residential, commercial, and industrial properties. We take pride in our ability to find the perfect match for buyers and connect sellers with interested parties, ensuring a seamless transaction experience.

Legal Advisory Services

Property transactions involve intricate legal procedures, and it's crucial to have the right guidance throughout the process. Amrawati Associates offers comprehensive legal advisory services, ensuring that all legal aspects related to property sales, purchases, and construction projects are handled efficiently. Our team of legal experts is well-versed in property laws and regulations, providing clients with peace of mind and minimizing any potential legal risks.
